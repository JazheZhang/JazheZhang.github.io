Who I am

About Me

Hey, I'm Jack Zhang, a CCIT student at the University of Toronto Mississauga. I got into product because I realized I had the power to change something tangible. Instead of being the person who complains about bad design, I decided to become the person who fixes it.

I'm working toward a career in UX/UI design and product management. I think about why products succeed or fail: not just how they look, but how they create real value for users and businesses alike. I've competed in accessibility case competitions, shipped designs under 4-hour time constraints, and built this site from scratch.

Outside of class, I'm constantly building, competing, and exploring. I believe the people who make the best products are the ones who combine creative empathy with commercial clarity.

"The best products solve problems people couldn't articulate themselves. My job is to find those problems before anyone else does."
